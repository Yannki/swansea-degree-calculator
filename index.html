Swansea University Degree Classification Calculator (Unofficial)

This is a personal project of a Computer Science student alumni from Swansea University. The website aim is to calculate predicted general Degree Classification of BSc, MSci Computer Science and other degrees if possible according to the information from a Swansea University website about calculating degree classification. The link of the website that the calculation is based on:

https://myuni.swansea.ac.uk/academic-life/academic-regulations/undergraduate-award-regulations/ug-assessment-regs/ug-assessment-regs-section-3/

The calculator was tested on 3 Year Initial Degree with real grades from Computer Science student. The deviation was about 1% from actual grades given by the University as a final Degree Classification. However, I do not guarantee the deviation and accuracy of the calculator as the testing sample was limited.

!!!DISCLAIMER: I do not guarantee the accuracy of the calculator!!!

How it exactly works

  1. Choose which type of course have you taken.
  2. Input the data of your grades and respective credits for modules
  3. S is a text field for a gap year grade
  4. Click on button CALCULATE
  5. See the result under the button

You can put your grades up until now to calculate the current average grade. This calculator was to be as universal as possible in mind.
